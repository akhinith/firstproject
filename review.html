THE PURSUIT OF HAPPINES

Gabriele Muccino

"The Pursuit of Happyness" is a biographical drama film based on the life of Chris Gardner. The story follows Gardner, played by Will Smith, as he struggles with homelessness while working as an unpaid intern in a competitive stockbroker training program. Despite numerous challenges, Gardner's determination and resilience lead to eventual success, earning him a full-time position and a chance at a better life for himself and his son. The film highlights themes of perseverance, the pursuit of one's dreams, and the bond between a father and son.

INTERSTELLAR

Christopher Nolan

"Interstellar" is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. Set in a dystopian future where Earth is suffering from a lack of resources, a former NASA pilot, played by Matthew McConaughey, embarks on a space mission to find a new habitable planet for humanity. The story explores themes of love, time dilation, and the human spirit, as the characters navigate through wormholes and encounter the effects of relativity. The film weaves together elements of physics and emotion, creating a visually stunning and intellectually engaging narrative that explores the survival of humanity in the face of environmental challenges
